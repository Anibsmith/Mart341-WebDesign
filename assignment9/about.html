About Pole Vaulting

Pole vaulting is a track and field event that combines strength, speed, and technique to vault over a high bar using a flexible pole. It has a rich history and remains one of the most exhilarating athletic challenges to this day. From beginners to professionals, pole vaulting offers an incredible mix of physicality and strategy.

This guide is designed to help you understand the basics of pole vaulting, from choosing the right pole to mastering advanced techniques. Whether you're new to the sport or looking to refine your skills, we've got you covered.

Lay's Potato Chips History

Lay's was founded in 1932 by Herman W. Lay. Initially sold in a small shop in Nashville, Tennessee, the company grew rapidly and was later purchased by Frito-Lay, which now operates globally.

Over the years, Lay's has become a leading snack brand, known for innovation, quality, and its wide variety of flavors.

Step-by-Step Guide to Pole Vaulting

Mastering the pole vault requires proper technique, strength, and practice. Follow these steps to get started:

  1. Choose the Right Pole: Select a pole that matches your weight and skill level.
  2. Learn the Grip: Hold the pole with your top hand near the top and your bottom hand spaced comfortably below.
  3. Practice the Approach: Develop a consistent run-up, focusing on speed and control.
  4. Plant the Pole: Plant the pole into the box at the end of the runway as you begin your jump.
  5. Execute the Takeoff: Push off the ground with your lead foot, using the pole for leverage.
  6. Swing and Invert: Swing your trail leg upward to invert your body and align with the pole.
  7. Clear the Bar: Propel your body over the bar with a controlled push-off from the pole.
  8. Land Safely: Land on your back or shoulders in the designated mat area to avoid injury.

Remember, practice regularly and seek guidance from experienced coaches to refine your technique.
